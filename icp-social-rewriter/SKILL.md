# ICP Social Rewriter

Rewrite content for one audience: tech-savvy, time-poor operators who want AI leverage without security risk or setup burden.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform and objective.
- Platform: Instagram, LinkedIn, or Twitter/X.
- Objective: awareness, trust-building, conversion, or launch.

2. Align message to ICP pains and outcomes.
- Pains: setup complexity, privacy risk anxiety, maintenance burden, tool fatigue.
- Outcomes: speed to value, control, reliability, peace of mind.

3. Build a stronger hook.
- Use one of these formats:
  - Pain-first: "Still losing weekends to self-hosting?"
  - Contrast: "OpenClaw power without infra chaos."
  - Outcome-first: "From setup stress to live workflow in hours."
  - Myth-breaker: "Self-hosting is not always the smartest move."

4. Rewrite with platform rules.

### Instagram
- 1-line hook, 3-6 short lines, high readability.
- Use simple language and emotional clarity.
- End with one CTA (comment, DM, or link-in-bio action).
- Optional hashtags: 3-8 relevant tags, no tag stuffing.

### LinkedIn
- Open with a specific operator pain, then a clear point of view.
- Use short paragraphs and concrete claims.
- Add trust signals: permissions model, revocable access, uptime/monitoring.
- End with one CTA question or invitation.

### Twitter/X
- First line must stand alone as the hook.
- Keep each sentence high-signal; remove filler.
- Prefer concrete phrasing over generic claims.
- End with one explicit action (reply, follow, DM, click).

5. Quality check before returning.
- Audience fit: sounds like it is written for privacy-first operators.
- Specificity: includes at least one concrete pain and one concrete outcome.
- Clarity: no jargon-heavy or vague statements.
- Conversion: includes exactly one primary CTA.

## Output Format

Return:
- Rewritten copy
- Hook options (3 variants)
- Why it works (2-4 bullets tied to ICP)
- Optional CTA alternatives (max 2)
