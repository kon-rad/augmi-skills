# Augmi ICP Reference

## Audience
Privacy-first power users: tech-savvy operators who want OpenClaw leverage without setup complexity, maintenance burden, or data risk.

## Core Segments
- Marketers and growth operators
- Founders and solopreneurs
- Consultants and agency owners
- Small-team operations leads / default tech person

## Top Pains
1. Setup complexity (servers, keys, permissions, updates)
2. Fear of data leaks and misconfiguration
3. No time to maintain secure infrastructure
4. Tool fatigue and context switching

## Desired Outcomes
- Productive in hours, not weeks
- Granular and revocable permissions
- Managed uptime, backups, updates
- Faster workflow output (search, summarize, create, automate)

## Message Priorities
1. Privacy and control
2. Reliability and trust
3. Time leverage and simplicity
4. Clear onboarding and support

## Core Positioning
OpenClaw power without the self-hosting headache: secure, managed, privacy-first setup with clear controls.
