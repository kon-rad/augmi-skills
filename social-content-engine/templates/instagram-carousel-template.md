# Instagram Carousel Template (v2)

## Structure

```markdown
# Instagram Carousel: {Topic}

## Slide 1 (Cover)
**Text:** {Bold, provocative hook -- 5-8 words max}
**Subtext:** {Optional 1-line context}

## Slide 2
**Text:** {Key stat or fact -- large text}
**Detail:** {1-2 sentence explanation}

## Slide 3 (List)
**Text:** {List headline}
**Items:**
1. {First key point}
2. {Second key point}
3. {Third key point}

## Slide 4 (Stat)
**Number:** {Large metric like 847% or $10K}
**Text:** {What the number represents}
**Detail:** {Source or context}

## Slide 5
**Text:** {The problem or challenge}
**Detail:** {Why it matters}

## Slide 6
**Text:** {The solution or shift happening}
**Detail:** {Evidence}

## Slide 7 (Quote)
**Quote:** {Impactful quote or testimonial}
**Attribution:** {-- Source name}

## Slide 8
**Text:** {What this means for you}
**Detail:** {Actionable takeaway}

## Slide 9 (CTA)
**Text:** {CTA -- "Deploy your own AI agent"}
**Subtext:** augmi.world
**Action:** Follow @augmi.world for more

---

## Caption

{2-3 sentence summary with engagement hook.
Ask a question to drive comments.}

Hashtags: #AIAgents #Web3 #BuildInPublic #TechTrends #AI

---

## Cover Image Prompt

{Detailed image generation prompt for 4:5 aspect ratio cover image.
Should be scroll-stopping, bold, visually striking.
Include: subject, style, colors, composition, mood.}
```

## Slide Types

Use type annotations in parentheses to control slide rendering:

| Type | When to Use | Key Fields |
|------|-------------|------------|
| `(Cover)` | Slide 1 always | Text, Subtext |
| _(none)_ | Standard content | Text, Detail |
| `(List)` | Tips, steps, points | Text, Items (numbered) |
| `(Stat)` | Data points, metrics | Number, Text, Detail |
| `(Quote)` | Testimonials, statements | Quote, Attribution |
| `(CTA)` / `(Final)` | Last slide always | Text, Subtext, Action |

## Writing Guidelines

- **7-10 slides** -- enough to tell a story, not so many people drop off
- **5-8 words per slide headline** -- designed for quick swiping
- **Large text dominates** -- detail text is secondary
- **Each slide is one idea** -- don't cram
- **Slide 1 must stop the scroll** -- bold claim, specific number, or provocative question
- **Use numbers** -- "5 Steps" not "Steps", "$10K" not "a lot"
- **Mix slide types** -- variety keeps engagement (don't use all content slides)
- **Final slide is always CTA** -- follow, visit, engage
- **Caption drives engagement** -- ask a question

### Cover Headline Formula
```
[Number] + [Specific Outcome] + [Timeframe/Context]
```
Examples:
- "7 AI Tools That Replaced My $50K Team"
- "5 Habits That 10x'd My Productivity in 90 Days"
- "The $0 Marketing Strategy That Got 100K Users"

### Slide Transitions
Create micro-cliffhangers to drive swipes:
- "But here's what most people miss..."
- "The surprising part?"
- "And the data backs it up..."

## Visual Style Notes

- **1080x1350px** portrait format (4:5 ratio, NOT square)
- Dark background (#09090b) on all slides
- Cyan (#06b6d4) accent for headlines and emphasis
- Bold sans-serif (Inter) for headlines
- Clean, minimal layout with accent lines and gradient strip
- Max 3 colors per slide
- No emojis in slide text (professional business style)

## Image Prompt Guidelines

The cover image prompt should produce a **4:5 portrait** image that:
- Is visually striking and thumb-stopping
- Uses dark backgrounds with vibrant accents
- Conveys the topic's energy (tech, future, innovation)
- Avoids text in the image (text is overlaid separately)
- Style: digital art, futuristic, clean
