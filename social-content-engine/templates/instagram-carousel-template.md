# Instagram Carousel Template

## Structure

```markdown
# Instagram Carousel: {Topic}

## Slide 1 (Cover)
**Text:** {Bold, provocative hook — 5-8 words max}
**Subtext:** {Optional 1-line context}

## Slide 2
**Text:** {Key stat or fact — large text}
**Detail:** {1-2 sentence explanation}

## Slide 3
**Text:** {Insight or "did you know"}
**Detail:** {Brief context}

## Slide 4
**Text:** {The problem or challenge}
**Detail:** {Why it matters}

## Slide 5
**Text:** {The solution or shift happening}
**Detail:** {Evidence}

## Slide 6
**Text:** {Data point or proof}
**Detail:** {Source attribution}

## Slide 7
**Text:** {What this means for you}
**Detail:** {Actionable takeaway}

## Slide 8 (Final)
**Text:** {CTA — "Deploy your own AI agent"}
**Subtext:** augmi.world
**Action:** Follow @augmi.world for more

---

## Caption

{2-3 sentence summary with engagement hook.
Ask a question to drive comments.}

Hashtags: #AIAgents #Web3 #BuildInPublic #TechTrends #AI

---

## Cover Image Prompt

{Detailed image generation prompt for 1:1 aspect ratio cover image.
Should be scroll-stopping, bold, visually striking.
Include: subject, style, colors, composition, mood.}
```

## Writing Guidelines

- **7-10 slides** — enough to tell a story, not so many people drop off
- **5-8 words per slide headline** — designed for quick swiping
- **Large text dominates** — detail text is secondary
- **Each slide is one idea** — don't cram
- **Slide 1 must stop the scroll** — bold claim or question
- **Final slide is always CTA** — follow, visit, engage
- **Caption drives engagement** — ask a question

## Visual Style Notes

- Dark background preferred (matches AUGMI brand)
- Purple/cyan accent colors
- Bold sans-serif for headlines
- Clean, minimal layout
- No more than 3 colors per slide

## Image Prompt Guidelines

The cover image prompt should produce a 1:1 image that:
- Is visually striking and thumb-stopping
- Uses dark backgrounds with vibrant accents
- Conveys the topic's energy (tech, future, innovation)
- Avoids text in the image (text is overlaid separately)
- Style: digital art, futuristic, clean
