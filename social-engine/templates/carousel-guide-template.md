# Instagram Carousel Style Guide

**Dimensions**: 1080x1350px per slide (4:5 portrait)
**Slides**: 6 slides per carousel
**Format**: Video carousel or image carousel
**Tools**: carousel-image-gen skill

---

## Visual Specifications

### Colors
- **Primary Brand**: [Your primary brand color]
- **Accent**: Cyan (#00D9FF)
- **Background**: Dark (choose based on brand)
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or dark contrast

### Typography
- **Headline**: Bold sans-serif, 48-56px
- **Body**: Regular sans-serif, 24-32px
- **Detail**: Light sans-serif, 18-24px

### Visual Elements
- Gradient strip at top (your primary color)
- Cyan accent lines for emphasis
- Numbered circles for steps (if applicable)
- "Swipe" arrow/cue on bottom right

---

## Slide Types

### Slide 1: Cover Slide
**Type**: Cover
- **Headline**: Bold, eye-catching
- **Subheading**: Additional context
- **Visuals**: Large, high-impact image
- **CTA Hint**: "Swipe →" or "Swipe to learn"

```markdown
## Slide 1 (Cover)
**Headline**: [Topic Title]
**Subheading**: [Hook/benefit in 5-7 words]
**Visuals**: [Sci-fi scene description for image generation]
```

### Slides 2-5: Content Slides
**Type**: Content / List / Stat / Quote
- **Title**: Specific point or concept
- **Details**: 2-3 sentences explaining the concept
- **Visual**: Supporting graphic or illustration

```markdown
## Slide 2 (Content)
**Title**: First Key Point
**Detail**: Explanation of why this matters

## Slide 3 (List)
**Title**: Three Key Elements
**Items**:
1. Element One
2. Element Two
3. Element Three

## Slide 4 (Stat)
**Title**: The Proof
**Number**: 92%
**Description**: What this statistic means

## Slide 5 (Quote)
**Quote**: "Powerful statement"
**Attribution**: - Expert Name
```

### Slide 6: CTA (Call-to-Action)
**Type**: CTA
- **Headline**: Main action
- **Details**: Where to go / what to do
- **Links**: Website, profile, link in bio
- **Visual**: Branded background

```markdown
## Slide 6 (CTA)
**Headline**: [Action - Learn More / Join Us / Try Now]
**Detail**: [Where they go / what happens]
**Link**: [Website or profile]
```

---

## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Authentic**: Sounds human, not AI
- **Educational**: Teach something valuable
- **Engaging**: Ask questions, make statements
- **Encouraging**: Positive, optimistic tone
- **Brand-Consistent**: Matches your voice

### Word Count
- **Headline**: 3-7 words
- **Subheading**: 5-10 words
- **Body text**: 15-25 words per slide
- **CTA**: 3-5 words

### Engagement Hooks
- Start with question: "Did you know...?"
- Lead with benefit: "This changed everything..."
- Use numbers: "3 ways to...", "92% of..."
- Create curiosity: "Swipe to see..."

## Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Headline** - Largest, most prominent
2. **Image/Visual** - Large, eye-catching
3. **Detail text** - Supporting information
4. **CTA** - Clear, actionable

### Consistency
- Same fonts across all 6 slides
- Same color palette
- Similar layout structure
- Branded header/footer areas

### Accessibility
- High contrast (white text on dark)
- Readable from phone (test at actual size)
- Captions for videos
- Clear hierarchy

### Engagement
- Numbered slides (1/6, 2/6, etc. on side)
- "Swipe →" cue on most slides
- Progressive reveal of information
- Value delivered on each slide (not just first)

## Examples by Topic Type

### Educational Topic (How-To)
1. Cover: "3 Steps to [Goal]"
2. Context: Why this matters
3. Step 1: How to do first thing
4. Step 2: How to do second thing
5. Step 3: How to do third thing
6. CTA: Try it, join community

### News/Announcement
1. Cover: "Breaking: [Headline]"
2. What: What happened
3. Why: Why it matters
4. Impact: How it affects you
5. Quote: Expert perspective
6. CTA: Learn more, discuss

### Comparison/Review
1. Cover: "[Option A] vs [Option B]"
2. Context: Why comparing
3. Aspect 1: Comparison
4. Aspect 2: Comparison
5. Verdict: Which wins and why
6. CTA: Try both, choose

### Story/Journey
1. Cover: "The story of [X]"
2. Beginning: Where it started
3. Conflict: The challenge
4. Turning Point: What changed
5. Outcome: How it resolved
6. CTA: Your story? Share

## Caption Guidelines

Each carousel should include a detailed caption:

```markdown
[Hook/Question]

[Main topic explained in 2-3 sentences]

What stood out to you? Comment below! 👇

[Relevant hashtags - 10-15 max]
#topic #socialmedia #growth
```

### Caption Formula
1. **Hook** (question, bold stat, or bold statement)
2. **Context** (2-3 sentences about the carousel)
3. **Call-to-action** (ask for comments or engagement)
4. **Hashtags** (10-15 relevant tags)
5. **Optional**: Emoji for visual interest

## Posting Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best Days**: Tuesday - Thursday
- **Best Times**: 11 AM - 1 PM (your audience timezone)
- **Frequency**: 2-3 carousels per week

### Captions
- **Length**: 125-200 characters before "more"
- **Engagement**: Ask questions, encourage comments
- **Authenticity**: Write like a human, use `/humanizer` skill
- **Hashtags**: 10-15 relevant, researched tags

### Engagement
- Respond to comments within 1 hour
- Pin top comment or your response
- Save re-engaging carousels for later highlighting
- Feature in Stories if high engagement

## Performance Targets

### Engagement Rate
- **Target**: >3% (likes + comments + saves / impressions)
- **Excellent**: 5-10%
- **Good**: 3-5%
- **Fair**: 1-3%
- **Low**: <1%

### Save Rate
- **Target**: 10% of engagements
- Indicates "valuable" content
- Higher saves = better reach

### Share Rate
- **Target**: 5-10% of engagements
- Sign of highly valuable content
- Drives discovery via Stories

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too much text** - Keep each slide scannable
❌ **Small fonts** - Should be readable on phone
❌ **Cluttered design** - Whitespace is valuable
❌ **Inconsistent branding** - Use same fonts, colors
❌ **No clear CTA** - Always tell them what to do
❌ **Generic content** - Specific is better than general
❌ **Poor image quality** - High-res images are essential
❌ **No hook** - First slide must grab attention
