# LinkedIn, Twitter & Video Style Guides

Quick reference guides for LinkedIn articles, Twitter threads, and short-form videos.

---

## LinkedIn Article & Image Guide

### Image Specifications
- **Size (Article)**: 1200x627px (1.91:1 landscape)
- **Size (Post)**: 1080x1080px (1:1 square)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Style**: Professional, corporate, thought leadership

### Article Image
- **Headline**: Clear, benefit-driven (5-7 words)
- **Visual**: Professional scene, abstract, or graph
- **Color**: Professional palette, high contrast
- **Text Coverage**: 20-30%

### Post Image
- **Type**: Quote graphic, stat, article teaser, or professional scene
- **Layout**: Centered, professional
- **Text**: Large, easy to read on mobile
- **Visual**: Minimal, professional aesthetic

### LinkedIn Caption Formula
```
[Hook] - Bold statement or question
[Value] - What you're sharing and why
[Detail] - 2-3 sentences of insight
[CTA] - What should they do? Read? Comment? Follow?
#hashtags (3-5 relevant)
```

### Best Practices
- **Posting**: Tuesday - Thursday, 8-10 AM
- **Length**: 300-1000 words for articles
- **Voice**: Professional, authoritative, helpful
- **Tone**: Expert but approachable
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant, researched
- **Emojis**: Minimal, strategic (2-3 max)

### Performance Targets
- **Engagement**: 100+ reactions per post
- **Comments**: 20+ meaningful comments
- **Shares**: 10+ shares
- **Click-Through**: >1.5% for links

## Twitter/X Thread Style Guide

### Content Specifications
- **Format**: Threads (5-15 tweets)
- **Char Limit**: 280 characters per tweet
- **Images**: Optional, 1200x675px (16:9)
- **Videos**: Optional, <15 seconds recommended

### Thread Structure
1. **Hook Tweet** (Tweet 1)
   - 180-240 characters
   - Question, bold claim, or intriguing statement
   - Set up the value

2. **Value Tweets** (Tweets 2-n-1)
   - Each point: 1-3 tweets
   - Short, punchy, easy to understand
   - Build on previous tweet
   - Optional: 1 image per point

3. **CTA Tweet** (Final tweet)
   - Call to action: Follow, Retweet, Reply
   - Ask for engagement
   - Link if appropriate
   - Tag relevant accounts

### Writing Formula
```
1/ [Hook - question or bold statement] 🧵

2/ [First point - benefit or insight]

3/ [Second point - evidence or example]

4/ [Third point - additional value]

5/ [Counterpoint or advanced tip]

6/ [Wrap-up & call to action]

Like & retweet if helpful
Follow for [topic] insights
Reply with [question]
```

### Thread Examples

**How-To Thread**:
1. Hook: "How to do X in 5 steps"
2-5. Each step with example
6. CTA: Try it, share results

**Hot Take Thread**:
1. Hook: "Unpopular opinion: [statement]"
2-4. Evidence for opinion
5. Counterpoint (show balance)
6. CTA: What do you think?

**News Thread**:
1. Hook: "Breaking: [headline]"
2-4. What, why, impact
5. Expert take/quote
6. CTA: Full analysis in [link]

### Twitter Caption Tips
- **Opening**: Start strong (hooks in first 100 chars)
- **Pacing**: Don't overwhelm; space out key points
- **Format**: Use line breaks, numbered lists
- **Links**: Save for final tweet or bio
- **Hashtags**: 2-3 per thread (use in final tweet)
- **Emojis**: 1-2 max per tweet

### Best Practices
- **Posting**: Monday & Wednesday, 9-10 AM
- **Frequency**: 3-5 threads per week
- **Engagement**: Reply to comments within 1 hour
- **Cross-Promotion**: Pin top-performing threads
- **Voice**: Authentic, conversational, human
- **Tone**: Opinionated but respectful

### Performance Targets
- **Retweets**: 50+ per thread
- **Likes**: 200+ per thread
- **Replies**: 20+ meaningful comments
- **Clicks**: 5%+ CTR for links

## Short-Form Video Style Guide

### Video Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds (optimal: 45 seconds)
- **Format**: MP4, H.264 codec
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Bitrate**: 5-8 Mbps
- **File Size**: <100MB

### Platforms
- **YouTube Shorts**: 60 seconds max
- **Instagram Reels**: 90 seconds max
- **TikTok**: 10 minutes max (but keep short)
- **Twitter/X**: 2 minutes 20 seconds max

### Video Structure

**Opening (0-3 seconds)**
- Hook: Question, bold statement, or surprising visual
- Retain attention immediately
- Text overlay: What will they learn?

**Body (3-45 seconds)**
- Sequential steps or story beats
- Visual changes every 3-5 seconds
- Cut to new angle/shot frequently
- Build towards payoff

**Closing (45-60 seconds)**
- Payoff: Resolution or final insight
- Call-to-action: Like, comment, follow
- Text overlay: What to do next

### Captions & Text

**Auto-Generate**:
- Use short-video-gen skill (includes auto-captions)
- Review for accuracy
- Correct any misheard words

**Manual Overlays**:
- Font: Bold sans-serif, high contrast
- Size: Large (readable at phone size)
- Placement: Top/middle/bottom (consistent)
- Duration: 2-3 seconds per text
- Color: White with dark shadow

**Captions Formula**:
- Line 1: Main text (5-8 words)
- Line 2: Secondary text (optional, 3-5 words)
- Font: 48-64px
- Contrast: White on dark background minimum

### Audio Strategy
- **Music**: Trending audio when available
- **Voiceover**: Clear, paced for reading
- **SFX**: 1-2 subtle effects only
- **Levels**: -1 to -3 dB headroom

### Content Types

**Educational (How-To)**
- Hook: "Learn X in 30 seconds"
- Body: Step-by-step demo
- Close: "Now you know"
- CTA: Save this, follow for more

**Entertaining**
- Hook: Surprising or funny moment
- Body: Build to punchline
- Close: Payoff moment
- CTA: Tag someone who needs this

**Inspiring**
- Hook: Problem or challenge
- Body: Journey or insight
- Close: Motivation or takeaway
- CTA: What will you do?

**News/Update**
- Hook: "Here's what's new..."
- Body: Explanation with visuals
- Close: Why it matters
- CTA: Learn more (link in bio)

### Filming Tips
- **Lighting**: Bright, even, no harsh shadows
- **Background**: Clean, on-brand, or interesting
- **Movement**: Keep camera still (or smooth pans)
- **Pace**: Fast edits (3-5 sec per scene)
- **Changes**: New scene/angle every 2-3 seconds

### Editing
- Use short-video-gen skill for automation
- Or edit manually (Adobe Premiere, CapCut)
- Cut out pauses and "ums"
- Maintain consistent pacing
- Test on phone (actual size)

### Captions & Accessibility
- Burned-in captions (visible in video)
- Auto-captions backup
- Descriptive text for visuals
- Include what/why/how

### Best Practices
- **Posting**: Friday-Sunday (peak short-video hours)
- **Frequency**: 1-3 per week per platform
- **Thumbnails**: First frame should be compelling
- **Hook**: 0.5-1 second hook critical
- **Completion**: Target >70% watch-through rate
- **Trending**: Use trending audio when relevant

### Performance Targets
- **Completion Rate**: >70% (users watch to end)
- **Click-Through**: 1%+ (for links)
- **Engagement**: 5-10% (likes + comments)
- **Shares**: 5% of views
- **Follower Growth**: 5-10% weekly

### Common Video Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Slow opening** - Hook fails at 0-2 seconds
❌ **Too much text** - Overwhelms viewer
❌ **Poor audio** - Muffled or inconsistent levels
❌ **Shaky camera** - Use stabilizer or tripod
❌ **No captions** - Many watch on mute
❌ **Long scenes** - Edit for pace
❌ **Bad thumbnail** - First frame matters
❌ **No call-to-action** - Tell them what to do

## Cross-Platform Checklist

### Before Publishing Any Content

**Brand Consistency**
- ☐ Colors match brand palette
- ☐ Fonts from approved stack
- ☐ Logo included (if applicable)
- ☐ Tone matches brand voice

**Quality Check**
- ☐ No spelling/grammar errors
- ☐ Images high resolution (2400px+ minimum)
- ☐ Text readable at thumbnail size
- ☐ Video plays without audio first (captions needed)

**Engagement Setup**
- ☐ Clear call-to-action
- ☐ Relevant hashtags researched
- ☐ Timing optimized for platform
- ☐ Links working (if applicable)

**Humanization**
- ☐ Ran through `/humanizer` skill
- ☐ Sounds like human, not AI
- ☐ Has personality and opinion
- ☐ Conversational tone
